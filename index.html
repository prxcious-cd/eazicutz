About Eazi Cutz

Ibadan's home of clean lines and honest service

Eazi Cutz started with one simple promise: every client leaves looking sharper than they arrived. Tucked in Awotan, Apete, our chairs have become a trusted stop for students, professionals and families across Ibadan who want a barbershop that takes grooming seriously — without losing the warmth of a neighbourhood spot.

From tapered fades to intricate dreadlock styling, every service is handled by experienced hands, with sterilized tools, fresh towels and an environment built for comfort. We don't just cut hair — we build confidence, one appointment at a time.
